NIGHT INSECTS

"Night Insects" is a project, which was inspired by my childhood memories of two things: butterfly art and various insects of the night. I am hoping that people's drawings, from this site, will help them recreate these memories.

PRESS THE "Y" KEY FOR YELLOW.

PRESS THE "B" KEY FOR BLUE.

PRESS THE "X" KEY TO SAVE YOUR CURRENT DRAWING.
