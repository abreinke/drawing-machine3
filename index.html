NIGHT INSECTS

"Night Insects" is something which was inspired by my childhood memories of two things:
butterfly art and various insects of the night. I am hoping that people will be able to recreate similar memories, through their drawings on this site.

Instructions for Creating the Drawings

THE FIRST COLOR IS RED. SIMPLY HOLD DOWN AND DRAG THE MOUSE TO START YOUR DRAWING.

PRESS THE "Y" KEY FOR YELLOW.

PRESS THE "B" KEY FOR BLUE.

PRESS THE "D" KEY TO GO BACK TO RED.

PRESS THE "X" KEY TO SAVE YOUR CURRENT DRAWING.
